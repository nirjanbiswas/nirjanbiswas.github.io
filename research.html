My research has been in the analysis of PDE, more specifically in the elliptic PDE. One part of my doctoral research is devoted to studying the weighted Sobolev inequalities with weights in various function spaces. The other part of my doctoral research focuses on studying the associated weighted eigenvalue problems for the p-Laplace operator with zero Dirichlet, Neumann, and Steklov boundary conditions. Later I have worked on the bifurcation theory for the non-linear Steklov eigenvalue problems. In my post doctoral research, I recently studied the positivity of the solutions for semipositone biharmonic problem. At present, I am interested in fractional (p,q)-Laplace operator.

Research Interests

  • Analysis of Elliptic Partial Differential Equations

  • Study of weighted non-linear eigenvalue problems

  • Hardy-Sobolev Inequalities with admissible Function spaces

  • Optimization of the first Dirichlet eigenvalue over rearrangement invariant classes and under the domain perturbations

  • Positivity of the solutions on the entire R^N for elliptic equations with semipositone nonlinearity

  • Spectrum analysis for double phase non-linear fractional problems

  • Regularity theory for non-local and mixed local non-local elliptic equations

  • Bifurcation theory for nonlinear elliptic eigenvalue problem
